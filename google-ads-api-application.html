Business Model:

Fretron is a B2B SaaS company providing Transportation Management System (TMS) software to enterprises in India. We run Google Ads campaigns to generate leads for our software products at fretron.com.

We advertise only for our own company and do not manage ads for any third parties or external clients.

Tool Access/Use:

Our internal marketing analytics tool will be used by Fretron's marketing team (2-3 employees) to:

  • View and analyze Google Ads campaign performance alongside other marketing channels (Meta Ads, email campaigns, HubSpot CRM)
  • Generate unified marketing reports combining data from multiple advertising platforms
  • Track lead generation metrics and calculate ROI across all paid channels

The tool is for internal use only and will not be accessible to external parties or clients.

Tool Design:

Our marketing tool has two main functions:

1. Reporting & Analytics:

  • Sync Google Ads data daily into our internal database
  • Display campaign and ad group performance metrics (impressions, clicks, conversions, cost)
  • Compare performance with other advertising platforms (Meta Ads)
  • Track lead attribution from ads to CRM deals in HubSpot

2. Campaign Management:

  • Create and manage campaigns programmatically based on our marketing calendar
  • Update ad copy and targeting based on performance data
  • Pause/enable campaigns based on budget allocation
  • Automate A/B testing of ad creatives

All API operations are performed only on our own Fretron ad account. We do not manage ads for third parties.

API Services Called:
  • GoogleAdsService (search/searchStream) - Pull campaign and ad group performance reports
  • CampaignService - Create, update, and manage campaigns
  • AdGroupService - Create and manage ad groups
  • AdGroupAdService - Create and manage ads within ad groups
  • Customer resource - Retrieve account information and settings
