## Pro Tips

1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** - Free 1-month trial gives advanced search
2. **Boolean Search** - Use quotes and OR operators for better results
3. **Company + "appointed" OR "joins"** - Find recent leadership changes
4. **Check press releases** - Company newsrooms often announce new hires
