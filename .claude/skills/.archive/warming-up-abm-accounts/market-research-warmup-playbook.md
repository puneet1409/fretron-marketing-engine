# Market Research Warmup Playbook (Full Funnel)

One of the most powerful ABM warmup tactics from Full Funnel methodology.

## Why Market Research Works

**Multiple Benefits in One Motion:**
- Engage buyers in co-creating valuable content
- Gain insights about challenges, needs, and goals
- Position yourself as industry thought leader
- Create content for distribution and nurturing
- Identify expansion opportunities with existing customers
- Reactivate stalled opportunities
- Build credibility through industry influencers

## Market Research Approach Options

| Approach | Example | Best For |
|----------|---------|----------|
| State of Industry | "State of Logistics in Manufacturing 2025" | Establishing authority |
| Trends Report | "7 Trends Reshaping Steel Logistics" | Forward-thinking positioning |
| Challenges Analysis | "Common B2B Logistics Challenges & Solutions" | Problem-focused content |
| Benchmarks | "Freight Cost Benchmarks: Steel Industry" | Data-driven credibility |

## 6-Week Market Research Campaign

### Week 1-2: Strategy & Setup

**Define Research Angle:**
- Choose topic that resonates with target accounts
- Ensure alignment with Fretron's value proposition
- Title should be specific to industry/segment

**Create Participant List:**
- Start with existing customers (easier yes, social proof)
- Add target accounts from ABM list
- Include industry influencers for reach

**Prepare Materials:**
- Research brief (see template below)
- Interview questions
- Survey as fallback option
- Calendar booking link

### Week 3-4: Outreach & Interviews

**Outreach Sequence:**
1. Personalized email invitation
2. LinkedIn message (if connected)
3. Follow-up email (3-5 days later)

**Interview Logistics:**
- 20-30 minutes via video call
- Record with permission
- Take detailed notes
- Offer survey as alternative

**Who to Target:**
- Champions from existing customers
- Engaged prospects from ABM warmup
- Industry thought leaders
- Peers of target contacts

### Week 5-6: Content & Activation

**Content Creation:**
- Compile insights into report
- Create visual assets and diagrams
- Pull quotable moments
- Build presentation deck

**Activation Options:**
1. Webinar presenting findings
2. Personalized solution follow-ups
3. Direct mail with printed report

## Interview Question Framework

### Challenge Questions
- "What are the biggest challenges you face with logistics/freight management?"
- "Which of these challenges has the highest priority this year?"
- "How does this challenge impact your organization day-to-day?"
- "What would solving this challenge mean for your team?"

### Current State Questions
- "How do you currently manage [specific process]?"
- "What platforms/tools are in your logistics stack?"
- "How do different teams access the data they need?"
- "Where do you see the biggest gaps?"

### Measurement Questions
- "How would you score your current logistics visibility (1-10)?"
- "How vital is freight cost optimization in your organization (1-10)?"
- "How frequently do you need real-time shipment data?"

### Prioritization Questions
- "How would you prioritize solving this challenge vs. other initiatives?"
- "What budget allocation exists for logistics technology?"
- "What timeline pressures exist for improvement?"

## Activation After Research

### Option 1: Webinar Presentation
- Present insights to all participants + target accounts
- Q&A session to engage further
- Follow-up with content hub access
- Great for scaling reach

### Option 2: Personalized Solutions
For accounts showing clear need:
- "Based on the challenges you mentioned, we can offer a mini-audit..."
- Personalized recommendations based on their interview
- 1:1 follow-up call

### Option 3: Direct Mail + Report
- Send printed report with personalized cover letter
- Include personalized solution summary
- Premium packaging for high-value accounts
- QR code to digital resources

## Content Hub Structure

After research, create a hub with:
- [ ] Research PDF (full report)
- [ ] Webinar recording
- [ ] Key insights infographic
- [ ] Related case studies
- [ ] ROI calculator
- [ ] Consultation booking link

**Track engagement** with the hub to identify hot prospects.

## Measuring Success

### Research Phase Metrics
- Participation rate (target: 20-30% of outreach)
- Interview completion rate
- Survey completion rate
- Quality of insights gathered

### Activation Phase Metrics
- Webinar registration rate
- Content hub engagement
- Follow-up meeting bookings
- Pipeline generated

### Long-term Impact
- Accounts moved to "engaged" status
- Relationships strengthened with participants
- Content reuse across channels
- Brand perception improvement
