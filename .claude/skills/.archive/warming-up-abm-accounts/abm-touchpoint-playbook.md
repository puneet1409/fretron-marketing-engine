# ABM Touchpoint Playbook

Detailed touchpoint sequences for warming up target accounts.

## The 90-Day Warmup Framework

### Phase Overview

```
PHASE 1: RESEARCH & LISTENING (Days 1-30)
Goal: Build intelligence, establish passive presence
Touchpoints: 8-12
Trust Level: 0 → 1

PHASE 2: LIGHT ENGAGEMENT (Days 31-60)
Goal: Create familiarity, demonstrate relevance
Touchpoints: 10-15
Trust Level: 1 → 2

PHASE 3: VALUE DELIVERY (Days 61-90)
Goal: Deliver tangible value, earn conversation
Touchpoints: 8-12
Trust Level: 2 → 3
```

---

## Phase 1: Research & Listening (Days 1-30)

### Week 1: Intelligence Gathering

**Day 1-2: Company Research**
- [ ] Review company website, annual reports
- [ ] Map organizational structure
- [ ] Identify recent news/announcements
- [ ] Note technology stack (if visible)
- [ ] Document logistics footprint (plants, warehouses)

**Day 3-4: Contact Mapping**
- [ ] Identify buying committee (LinkedIn Sales Navigator)
- [ ] Map reporting relationships
- [ ] Find key personas: VP Ops, Logistics Head, CFO, CIO
- [ ] Document each contact's background, interests

**Day 5-7: Social Listening**
- [ ] Follow key contacts on LinkedIn
- [ ] Note their content themes and interests
- [ ] Identify shared connections
- [ ] Look for engagement patterns

### Week 2: Passive Presence

**Day 8-10: Initial LinkedIn Activity**
```
Action: Follow company page and 3-5 key contacts
Visibility: Low (they may notice follower notification)
Trust Building: Shows interest without intrusion
```

**Day 11-14: Content Observation**
```
Action: Read and observe their posts (no engagement yet)
Note: Topics they discuss, challenges they mention
Intel: Build understanding of their priorities
```

### Week 3-4: Light Social Signals

**Day 15-21: Start Engaging**
```
Action: Like 2-3 posts from key contacts
Frequency: One like per person per week (not spam)
Content: Only genuinely relevant posts
```

**Day 22-30: Thoughtful Reactions**
```
Action: React to company news/announcements
Optional: Like comments from their team
Signal: "We're paying attention to your success"
```

## Phase 2: Light Engagement (Days 31-60)

### Week 5: Meaningful Comments

**Day 31-35: First Comments**
```
Action: Leave thoughtful comment on contact's post
Quality: Add value, share perspective, ask question
Avoid: Generic ("Great post!") or self-promotional

Example Comment:
"Interesting point about carrier consolidation. We've seen
similar patterns in steel logistics - the challenge is
balancing cost efficiency with delivery flexibility.
Curious how you're handling peak season capacity?"
```

**Day 36-40: Continue Engagement**
```
Action: 1-2 more quality comments across contacts
Pattern: Establish yourself as thoughtful contributor
Intel: Their response style tells you about receptiveness
```

### Week 6: Connection Requests

**Day 41-45: Send Connections**
```
Action: Connect with 2-3 key contacts
Message: Personalized, reference specific content

Template:
"Hi [Name], I've been following your posts on [topic] -
particularly liked your point about [specific insight].
I work with [industry] companies on logistics challenges.
Would love to connect and learn from your perspective."
```

**Day 46-50: Continue Engagement Post-Connect**
```
Action: Like their content, occasional comments
Signal: You connected for dialogue, not just network building
```

### Week 7-8: Initial Value Signals

**Day 51-55: Share Relevant Content**
```
Action: Share content relevant to their industry/challenges
Tag: Occasionally tag contacts if highly relevant
Format: "Thought this might interest those in [industry] -
[brief summary of why]"
```

**Day 56-60: First DM (Low-Key)**
```
Action: Send brief, value-focused DM
Ask: Nothing - just give

Template:
"Hi [Name], saw your post about [topic]. We just published
some benchmark data on [related topic] that might be useful.
Happy to share if you're interested - no strings attached."
```

## Phase 3: Value Delivery (Days 61-90)

### Week 9: Personalized Value

**Day 61-65: Custom Insight**
```
Action: Create/share something specific to their situation
Examples:
- Industry benchmark with their segment highlighted
- Article about challenge they've mentioned
- Introduction to peer (if appropriate)

DM Template:
"Hi [Name], I came across this [content] and immediately
thought of your [specific challenge/post they mentioned].
Thought it might be useful for what you're working on."
```

### Week 10: Deeper Engagement

**Day 66-70: Ask for Perspective**
```
Action: Seek their input (flattering, not selling)

DM Template:
"Hi [Name], I'm putting together some insights on [topic
they care about]. Given your experience with [their
situation], I'd value your perspective. Would you have
10 minutes for a quick call? I'm trying to understand
the [industry] view better."
```

**Day 71-75: Continue Value Flow**
```
Action: Share another relevant resource
Pattern: 3:1 ratio - three gives before any ask
Content: Case study, benchmark, article, introduction
```

### Week 11-12: Transition to Conversation

**Day 76-80: Warm Outreach**
```
Action: First mention of potential business relevance
Positioning: Helpful, not pushy

DM Template:
"Hi [Name], based on our exchanges, it seems like
[specific challenge] is on your radar. We've been
helping [industry] companies with exactly this.

Not sure if there's a fit, but would you be open to
a brief call to explore? No pressure - happy to just
share what we're seeing in the market."
```

**Day 81-85: Follow-Up If No Response**
```
Action: Gentle follow-up with additional value

Template:
"Hi [Name], just wanted to follow up on my note. Either
way, thought you might find this interesting - [relevant
content]. Let me know if a quick chat makes sense."
```

**Day 86-90: Final Phase 3 Effort**
```
Action: Clear ask or graceful close

If interest shown:
"Great to hear! Would [specific day/time] work? Here's
my calendar: [Link]"

If no response:
"I've shared a few things over the past weeks - hope
they've been useful. I'll ease off the follow-ups, but
if logistics visibility ever becomes a priority, I'm
here. Feel free to reach out anytime."
```

## Multi-Channel Coordination

### Coordinating LinkedIn + Email

| Day | LinkedIn | Email | Combined Effect |
|-----|----------|-------|-----------------|
| 1-30 | Research, follows | None | Build intelligence |
| 31-45 | Comments, connects | None | Establish presence |
| 46-60 | DMs with value | Optional: Industry insight | Dual visibility |
| 61-75 | Deep engagement | Value email (benchmark) | Reinforce relevance |
| 76-90 | Warm ask | Warm email sequence | Multichannel momentum |

### Email Coordination Templates

**Email (Day 46) - Parallel to LinkedIn Engagement**
```
Subject: Quick [industry] insight

Hi [Name],

I've been following your work on LinkedIn - appreciated your
recent thoughts on [topic they posted about].

Thought this might be relevant: [link to industry insight/
benchmark that relates to their interests]

No agenda - just thought it might be useful given what you're
working on.

Best,
[Signature]
```

**Email (Day 70) - Reinforcing LinkedIn Value**
```
Subject: Following up on [topic]

Hi [Name],

I shared this on LinkedIn but wanted to make sure you saw it:
[specific resource relevant to them]

Based on what you've shared about [their situation], I think
the section on [specific part] might be particularly relevant.

Happy to discuss if helpful.

Best,
[Signature]
```

## Account Warmth Scoring

### Scoring Criteria

| Signal | Points | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Accepted LinkedIn connection | +10 | Basic receptiveness |
| Responded to DM | +15 | Active engagement |
| Engaged with our content | +10 | Showing interest |
| Opened email | +5 | Passive awareness |
| Clicked email link | +10 | Active interest |
| Website visit (tracked) | +15 | High intent |
| Downloaded content | +20 | Strong interest |
| Replied to email | +20 | Conversation started |
| Agreed to call | +25 | Sales-ready |
| Multiple contacts engaged | +15 | Account-level interest |

### Warmth Thresholds

| Score | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 0-20 | Cold | Continue Phase 1-2 |
| 21-40 | Warming | Continue Phase 2-3 |
| 41-60 | Warm | Transition to Phase 3 |
| 61-80 | Hot | Sales outreach appropriate |
| 81+ | Very Hot | Immediate sales engagement |

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Too Fast
- Sending connection request on Day 1
- Pitching product in first DM
- Asking for meeting before building rapport

### Too Generic
- "Great post!" comments
- Mass connection requests
- Template DMs without personalization

### Too Aggressive
- Multiple touches per day
- Following up too quickly
- Ignoring "no" signals

### Too Passive
- Only liking, never commenting
- Never transitioning to conversation
- 90+ days with no progress
