## Newsletter Strategy

### Purpose by Newsletter Type

| Newsletter Type | Audience | Frequency | Primary Goal |
|-----------------|----------|-----------|--------------|
| Industry Insights | Prospects + Customers | Weekly | Thought leadership |
| Customer Updates | Active customers | Monthly | Retention, expansion |
| Product Updates | All subscribers | As needed | Feature adoption |
| Digest/Roundup | Prospects | Weekly | Lead nurturing |

### Core Metrics

| Metric | Benchmark | Action Threshold |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| Open Rate | 25-35% | <20% = Improve subject lines |
| Click Rate | 3-5% | <2% = Improve content/CTAs |
| Unsubscribe Rate | <0.5% | >1% = Review frequency/relevance |
| Reply Rate | 0.5-1% | Positive signal, encourage more |

## Newsletter Writing Guidelines

### Subject Lines

**Principles**:
- Specific > Vague (use numbers when possible)
- Benefit-focused > Feature-focused
- 40-50 characters ideal
- Test with preview text in mind

**Formulas**:
- [Number] + [Topic] + [Benefit]
- [Question that resonates with pain]
- [Unexpected insight] + [Why it matters]
- [Time-sensitive element] + [Clear value]

### Body Copy

**Principles**:
- One idea per section
- Short paragraphs (3-4 lines max)
- Conversational tone (write like you talk)
- Clear hierarchy (scannable)

**Structure Rules**:
- Most important content first
- One CTA per section (max)
- White space is your friend
- Mobile-first formatting

### CTAs

**Principles**:
- Action-oriented (verb-first)
- Specific (not "Learn More")
- Benefit-clear ("Get the Guide" vs "Download")
- Visually distinct (button or linked text)

**Examples**:
- "Read the Case Study â†’"
- "Get the Calculator"
- "Register Now"
- "See It in Action"

| Segment | Definition | Newsletter Approach |
|---------|------------|---------------------|
| Highly Engaged | Opens 80%+, clicks regularly | Feature new content, advanced tips |

## Testing Framework

### A/B Test Priority

1. **Subject lines** (biggest impact on opens)
2. **Send time** (day of week, time of day)
3. **From name** (personal vs. brand)
4. **CTA placement** (top vs. bottom)
5. **Content length** (concise vs. comprehensive)

### Test Methodology

- Test one variable at a time
- Minimum 500 recipients per variant
- Wait 48 hours for full results
- Document learnings for future reference
