# Amplifying LinkedIn Presence for Fretron

Build organic LinkedIn presence that generates pipeline and builds trust.

## When to Use This Skill

- Developing LinkedIn content strategy
- Creating executive thought leadership
- Coordinating team/employee advocacy
- Planning engagement-based outreach
- Converting LinkedIn engagement to pipeline

## Why LinkedIn Matters

**Key Insight**: LinkedIn engagement-based outreach drives 10% of pipeline for top performers.

**The Opportunity**:
- Decision-makers are active (Logistics heads, CFOs, VP Ops)
- Organic reach still works (unlike other platforms)
- Trust builds through consistent, valuable content
- Engagement signals buying intent

**Trust Philosophy**: LinkedIn is a trust-building platform, not a sales channel. Lead with value, earn permission to sell.

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars for Fretron

| Pillar | Description | Examples |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| Industry Insights | Trends, challenges, observations | "3 things I'm seeing in manufacturing logistics..." |
| Customer Stories | Anonymized wins, lessons | "Helped a steel manufacturer cut freight costs 15%..." |
| Behind the Scenes | Product, team, culture | "What we learned building our load optimizer..." |
| Contrarian Takes | Challenge assumptions | "Why most TMS implementations fail (and how to avoid it)..." |
| Tactical Tips | Actionable advice | "Quick framework for freight cost benchmarking..." |

### Content Mix (Weekly)

**Recommended Cadence**: 3-5 posts per week for executives

| Day | Content Type | Goal |
|-----|--------------|------|
| Mon | Industry insight | Thought leadership |
| Wed | Customer story / Use case | Credibility |
| Fri | Personal take / Reflection | Authenticity |

**Optional Adds**:
- Tuesday: Tactical tip
- Thursday: Behind the scenes

### Content Formats

**Text Posts (Highest Engagement)**:
- Hook in first 2 lines
- 800-1200 characters optimal
- Use line breaks for readability
- End with question or CTA

**Carousel Posts**:
- 5-10 slides
- One key point per slide
- Visual + minimal text
- Strong first slide hook

**Video Posts**:
- Under 2 minutes
- Vertical or square format
- Subtitles required
- Personal > polished

**Document Posts**:
- PDF slides (carousel alternative)
- Guides, frameworks, templates
- Gated feel, ungated content

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Engagement Routine (15-30 min)

**Morning (10 min)**:
1. Check notifications, respond to comments
2. Engage with 3-5 posts from target accounts
3. Like/comment on customer posts

**Evening (5-10 min)**:
1. Respond to afternoon comments
2. Engage with 2-3 industry posts
3. Check connection requests

### Engagement Quality

**Good Comment** (adds value):
```
"Great point about [specific thing]. We've seen similar -
[add your perspective or data]. One thing I'd add is
[additional insight]."
```

**Bad Comment** (generic):
```
"Great post!"
"So true!"
"Thanks for sharing!"
```

### Engagement-to-Outreach Conversion

**Step 1: Identify Engaged Prospects**
- Track who likes/comments on your posts
- Note target account employees
- Watch for repeat engagers

**Step 2: Warm Connection**

## Full Funnel ABM on LinkedIn

From Full Funnel methodology: LinkedIn is the primary channel for B2B ABM.

### The Full Funnel LinkedIn Approach

**Key Stats:**
- 2/3 of Full Funnel's clients discover them via LinkedIn
- 11-12 million impressions/year from consistent posting
- 95% of clients from inbound (driven by LinkedIn thought leadership)

### Content Approach (Fletch PMM Style)

**What Makes Content Work:**
- Practical and immediately applicable (frameworks they can use today)
- Distinctive visual style (recognizable in feed)
- Polarizing/opinionated (drives engagement)
- Generous with frameworks (free value builds trust)

**Content Pillars:**
1. **Framework Shares**: Visual frameworks and canvases
2. **Myth Busting**: Contrarian takes on common practices
3. **Before/After Examples**: Show transformation
4. **Case Studies**: Real results (anonymized if needed)

### LinkedIn ABM Warmup Integration

**For Tier 1 Target Accounts:**
1. Follow 3-5 key contacts from each account
2. Engage authentically with their content (2-3 weeks)
3. Connection request with context
4. Value-first DM (no pitch)
5. Soft conversation ask (after engagement)

**Key Insight**: The goal is not to sell on LinkedIn. It's to build familiarity so when you reach out, they recognize you.
