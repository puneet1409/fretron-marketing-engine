# Researching Keywords for Fretron

Find SEO keywords and topics that drive qualified organic traffic.

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning content calendar topics
- Finding high-intent keywords for landing pages
- Analyzing what competitors rank for
- Identifying geo-specific opportunities (India focus)
- Mapping keywords to buyer journey stages

## Keyword Research Framework

### Step 1: Seed Keywords
Start with core terms your buyers search.

**Fretron Seed Keywords:**
- TMS / Transport Management System
- Freight management software
- Logistics software India
- Fleet tracking software
- Dispatch management
- Freight cost reduction
- OTIF improvement
- E-way bill software

### Step 2: Expand with Modifiers

**Intent Modifiers:**
- [keyword] + software/tool/platform
- [keyword] + for [industry]
- best [keyword]
- [keyword] + pricing/cost
- [keyword] + vs [competitor]
- how to [keyword]

**India/Geo Modifiers:**
- [keyword] + India
- [keyword] + Mumbai/Delhi/Bangalore
- [keyword] + Indian companies

**Industry Modifiers:**
- [keyword] + for manufacturing
- [keyword] + for FMCG
- [keyword] + for cement/steel

### Step 3: Categorize by Intent

| Intent | Example Keywords | Content Type |
|--------|------------------|--------------|
| **Informational** | "what is TMS" / "how to reduce freight costs" | Blog posts, guides |
| **Commercial Investigation** | "best TMS India" / "TMS comparison" | Comparison pages, reviews |
| **Transactional** | "TMS software pricing" / "freight software demo" | Landing pages, pricing |
| **Navigational** | "Fretron login" / "Fretron demo" | Product pages |

## Keyword Research Tools

- **Google Search Console** - What you already rank for
- **Google Autocomplete** - Real search suggestions
- **Answer The Public** - Question-based keywords
- **Ubersuggest (limited)** - Basic metrics

### Paid Tools
- **Ahrefs** - Competitor analysis, keyword difficulty
- **SEMrush** - Comprehensive keyword data
- **Moz** - Keyword explorer

### Process Using Free Tools
1. Enter seed keyword in Google Keyword Planner
2. Export keyword ideas
3. Filter by relevance to Fretron
4. Check autocomplete for variations
5. Look at "People Also Ask" for question keywords
6. Review competitor pages for keyword ideas

## Keyword Prioritization Matrix

Score each keyword (1-5) on:

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Volume** | 2x | Monthly search volume |
| **Intent Fit** | 3x | How well does searcher match Fretron buyer |
| **Competition** | 2x | How hard to rank (inverse) |
| **Business Value** | 3x | How valuable is this visitor |

**Priority Score** = (Volume × 2) + (Intent × 3) + (Competition × 2) + (Value × 3)

### Quick Prioritization

**Prioritize:**
- Long-tail keywords (easier to rank, higher intent)
- Problem-specific terms (people ready to solve)
- India-specific modifiers (less competition)
- Industry-specific terms (more qualified)

**Deprioritize:**
- Generic head terms ("logistics" - too competitive)
- Very low volume (<50/month unless very high intent)
- Terms where intent doesn't match (B2C logistics, etc.)

## Content Mapping

Map keywords to content types and funnel stages:

### Top of Funnel (Awareness)
**Keywords**: "what is...", "how to...", "why...", "[problem]"
**Content**: Blog posts, guides, educational content
**Example**: "How to reduce freight costs in Indian logistics"

### Middle of Funnel (Consideration)
**Keywords**: "best...", "...software", "...comparison", "...vs..."
**Content**: Comparison pages, case studies, detailed guides
**Example**: "Best TMS software for mid-market manufacturers in India"

### Bottom of Funnel (Decision)
**Keywords**: "...pricing", "...demo", "...free trial", "[brand]"
**Content**: Landing pages, pricing pages, demo request
**Example**: "TMS software demo" / "Fretron pricing"

## Competitor Keyword Analysis

### Process
1. Identify top 3-5 competitors ranking for your keywords
2. Use Ahrefs/SEMrush to see their top organic pages
3. Find keywords they rank for that you don't
4. Identify content gaps (topics they haven't covered well)

### Competitors to Analyze
- Direct TMS competitors (local and global)
- Logistics content publishers
- Industry blogs ranking for your keywords
