## Keyword Research Process

### Step 1: Seed Keyword Generation

**Sources**:
1. Sales call transcripts (what prospects ask)
2. Customer support tickets (problem language)
3. Industry publications (terminology)
4. Competitor websites (their targeting)
5. LinkedIn discussions (real conversations)

**Seed Categories**:
```
Primary: tms, transportation management, logistics software
Secondary: freight, shipment, dispatch, carrier
Pain Points: tracking, visibility, cost, reconciliation
Industries: steel, cement, manufacturing, fmcg
Geography: india, indian
```

---

### Step 2: Keyword Expansion

**Tools**:
- Google Keyword Planner (volume, CPC)
- Ahrefs/SEMrush (competition, SERP analysis)
- Google Search (autocomplete, related searches)
- Answer the Public (question-based queries)

**Expansion Methods**:

1. **Modifier Addition**:
```
Base: tms software
Expanded:
- tms software india
- tms software for manufacturing
- best tms software
- tms software pricing
- cloud tms software
```

2. **Question Variants**:
```
Base: freight cost reduction
Questions:
- how to reduce freight costs
- what is freight cost optimization
- why is freight cost important
- who needs freight management
```

3. **Industry + Problem**:
```
Pattern: [industry] + [problem]
Results:
- steel industry logistics challenges
- cement freight cost management
- fmcg distribution problems
```

---

### Step 3: Keyword Qualification

**Scoring Matrix** (1-5 each):

| Criteria | Weight | Description |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| Search Volume | 20% | Monthly searches |
| Competition | 25% | Difficulty to rank |
| Intent Match | 30% | Alignment with Fretron ICP |
| Business Value | 25% | Revenue potential if ranked |

**Qualification Questions**:
1. Does this keyword attract our ICP?
2. Can we create better content than current SERP?
3. What's the conversion potential?
4. How does it fit our content strategy?

---

### Step 4: SERP Analysis

**For Each Priority Keyword, Analyze**:

1. **Current SERP**:
   - Who ranks in top 10?
   - What content types (blog, product, comparison)?
   - Featured snippets present?
   - People Also Ask questions?

2. **Content Gap**:
   - What's missing from top results?
   - Can we provide unique angle?
   - Do we have data/proof others don't?

3. **Difficulty Assessment**:
   - Domain authority of ranking sites
   - Content quality of top results
   - Age of ranking content
   - Backlink profiles

---

### Step 5: Keyword Mapping

**Map Keywords to Content Types**:

| Keyword Intent | Content Type | Example |
|---------------|--------------|---------|
| Navigational | Homepage, Feature pages | "fretron tms" |
| Informational | Blog posts, Guides | "what is tms" |
| Commercial | Comparison, Reviews | "best tms india" |
| Transactional | Landing pages | "tms demo" |

## Search Intent Patterns

### Transactional Intent Signals
- "buy", "pricing", "cost", "demo", "trial"
- Brand + product terms
- "best" + category
- Location modifiers

### Commercial Investigation Signals
- "vs", "compare", "alternative"
- "review", "rating"
- Year modifiers ("2025")
- "top", "best"

### Informational Intent Signals
- "what is", "how to", "why"
- "guide", "tutorial", "example"
- "benefits", "advantages"
- Question format

### Navigational Intent Signals
- Brand name
- Product name
- "[Brand] login", "[Brand] support"
