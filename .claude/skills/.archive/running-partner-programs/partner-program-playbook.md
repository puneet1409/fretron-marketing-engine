## Referral Partner Program

### Program Structure

**Tiers**:

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Registered** | Sign agreement | 5% referral fee, basic training |
| **Silver** | 2+ referrals/year | 7% fee, co-marketing support |
| **Gold** | 5+ referrals/year | 10% fee, priority support, MDF |

### Referral Process

```
1. REGISTRATION
   Partner signs agreement
   Completes basic training
   Gets partner portal access

2. LEAD SUBMISSION
   Partner submits lead via portal
   Provides context and warm intro
   Lead assigned to sales

3. QUALIFICATION
   Sales qualifies within 48 hours
   Partner notified of status
   Lead tracked in system

4. SALES PROCESS
   Regular updates to partner
   Partner involvement as needed
   Joint customer meetings

5. CLOSE & PAYOUT
   Deal closes
   Referral fee calculated
   Payment within 30 days
```

### Referral Fee Structure

| Deal Size | Registered | Silver | Gold |
|-----------|------------|--------|------|
| <₹10L ARR | 5% of Year 1 | 7% of Year 1 | 10% of Year 1 |
| ₹10-25L ARR | 5% of Year 1 | 7% of Year 1 | 10% of Year 1 |
| >₹25L ARR | 5% of Year 1 | 7% of Year 1 | 10% of Year 1 + bonus |

**Payment Terms**:
- Paid on customer payment (not contract signature)
- First year only (not recurring)
- Net 30 days after customer pays

### Referral Partner Enablement

**Training Modules**:
1. Fretron Overview (30 min) - Product, ICP, value proposition
2. Qualification Criteria (15 min) - What makes a good referral
3. How to Position (20 min) - Talking points, objection handling
4. Portal Training (15 min) - How to submit and track

**Materials Provided**:
- One-pager (PDF)
- Qualification checklist
- Email templates for introductions
- Case studies (1 per industry)

## Reseller/SI Partner Program

### Partner Tiers

| Tier | Requirements | Margin/Benefits |
|------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Authorized** | Certified team (1), 1+ deal | 15% margin, basic support |
| **Premier** | Certified team (2+), 3+ deals | 20% margin, deal reg, MDF |
| **Strategic** | Dedicated practice, 5+ deals | 25% margin, joint planning, exec sponsor |

### Certification Requirements

**Sales Certification**:
- Complete sales training (4 hours)
- Pass assessment (80%+)
- Annual recertification

**Technical Certification**:
- Complete implementation training (8 hours)
- Shadow 1 implementation
- Pass technical assessment
- Annual recertification

### Deal Registration

**Process**:
```
1. Partner submits deal via portal
   - Account info
   - Opportunity details
   - Expected close date
   - Competition

2. Fretron reviews within 48 hours
   - No conflict check
   - Opportunity validation
   - Registration approval/denial

3. If approved:
   - 90-day protection
   - Partner-led sales process
   - Fretron support as needed

4. Extensions:
   - Request before expiration
   - Valid reason required
   - 30-day extension max
```

### Partner Enablement

**Onboarding (First 30 Days)**:
- Day 1-7: Sales certification
- Day 8-14: Technical certification
- Day 15-21: Shadow deal/implementation
- Day 22-30: First independent opportunity

**Ongoing Enablement**:
- Monthly partner webinars
- Quarterly product updates
- Annual partner summit
- Dedicated partner manager (Premier+)

**Resources**:
- Partner portal (leads, collateral, training)
- Demo environment access
- Sales playbook
- Implementation guides
- Co-branded templates

## Partner Marketing

### Co-Marketing Activities

| Activity | Investment | Partner Contribution | Fretron Contribution |
|----------|------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Joint webinar | Low | Content, promotion | Platform, promotion |
| Co-branded content | Low | Expert input | Writing, design |
| Joint event | Medium | Attendance, hosting | Content, promotion |
| Case study | Low | Customer intro | Production |
| MDF campaign | Variable | Execution | Funding |

### MDF (Market Development Funds)

**Eligibility**: Premier and Strategic partners

**Allocation**:
- 2-5% of partner-influenced revenue
- Quarterly allocation based on performance
- Use-it-or-lose-it per quarter

**Approved Uses**:
- Joint events
- Co-branded content
- Targeted advertising
- Customer marketing

**Approval Process**:
1. Partner submits MDF request
2. Fretron reviews activity and ROI
3. Approval/feedback within 5 days
4. Partner executes, submits proof
5. Reimbursement within 30 days
