# STEEL INDUSTRY PLAYBOOK

## Industry Context

### Market Overview

**India Steel Industry**:
- Production: ~120 MT annually (world's 2nd largest)
- Major players: Tata Steel, JSW, SAIL, Jindal, AM/NS, JSPL
- Growing mid-market: 50+ secondary producers
- Logistics spend: 8-12% of revenue

**Logistics Complexity**:
- Plant-to-port-to-customer multi-leg journeys
- Weight-based reconciliation challenges
- OEM customer delivery compliance (penalties)
- High detention at ports and customer sites
- Rail vs. road optimization
- Seasonal demand patterns

### Pain Points (Priority Order)

| Pain Point | Impact | Fretron Solution |
|------------|--------|------------------|
| Tracking visibility | 500+ calls/day | Real-time tracking |
| OEM delivery penalties | ₹50L-2Cr/year | ETA accuracy, alerts |
| Detention at ports | 30-40% of hidden costs | Turnaround tracking |
| Freight reconciliation | 45+ day cycles | Automated matching |
| Weight discrepancies | 2-5% leakage | Weight-based audit |
| Rate compliance | 15-20% leakage | Contract enforcement |

### Buying Triggers

**High-Intent Triggers**:
- OEM audit/penalty notice received
- New capacity expansion (plant, port)
- Leadership change (VP Logistics, SCM Head)
- Competitor implementing TMS
- Export volume growth

**Medium-Intent Triggers**:
- Cost reduction mandate from CFO
- Customer complaints about visibility
- M&A activity (integration needs)
- ERP upgrade project
- Sustainability reporting requirements

### Primary Value Proposition

**For Steel**: "Control the complexity of steel logistics from plant to port to customer."

### Key Messages

1. **Visibility Message**: "See every MT from dispatch to delivery—without making a single tracking call."

2. **Cost Message**: "Steel companies find 15-25% freight savings hiding in detention, demurrage, and rate leakage."

3. **Compliance Message**: "Hit OEM delivery windows and avoid penalties with 95%+ ETA accuracy."

### Proof Points

- [Steel Customer A]: 85% reduction in tracking calls
- [Steel Customer B]: ₹1.2 Cr saved on detention in 6 months
- [Steel Customer C]: OEM penalty reduction from ₹45L to ₹3L/quarter

### Industry-Specific Terminology

**Use These Terms**:
- MT (metric ton), not "shipments"
- Dispatch, not "shipping"
- Rake (for rail), not "train"

- Weighbridge reconciliation

# CEMENT INDUSTRY PLAYBOOK

## Industry Context

### Market Overview

**India Cement Industry**:
- Production: ~380 MT capacity
- Major players: UltraTech, Ambuja, ACC, Shree, Dalmia, Ramco
- Growing mid-market: Regional players expanding
- Logistics spend: 25-35% of total cost (highest cost component)

**Logistics Complexity**:
- Multi-plant coordination (grinding units)
- Extensive dealer distribution network
- Extreme seasonal peaks (construction season)
- Vehicle turnaround time pressure
- Bulk vs. bag split optimization
- Last-mile dealer delivery

### Pain Points (Priority Order)

| Pain Point | Impact | Fretron Solution |
|------------|--------|------------------|
| Multi-plant dispatch | Suboptimal allocation | Centralized visibility |
| Dealer delivery SLAs | Lost sales, complaints | Delivery tracking |
| Vehicle TAT | Fleet underutilization | Turnaround optimization |
| Peak season chaos | Missed revenue | Capacity planning |
| Freight cost volatility | Margin pressure | Rate management |
| Dealer visibility demands | Relationship strain | Customer portal |

### Buying Triggers

**High-Intent Triggers**:
- New grinding unit commissioning
- Dealer complaints escalating
- Peak season failures (previous year)
- Fleet optimization project
- Distribution network expansion

**Medium-Intent Triggers**:
- Cost reduction pressure
- Competitor improving delivery
- Digital transformation initiative
- New leadership in SCM
- Working capital optimization focus

- Freshness/expiry management

- Returns handling

|------------|--------|------------------|

| Promotional logistics | Missed sales | Demand surge handling |
| Distribution visibility | Customer complaints | Real-time tracking |
