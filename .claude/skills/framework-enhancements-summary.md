## Market Research Warmup Playbook (Full Funnel)

This is a powerful ABM technique from the Full Funnel transcript that should be added to `warming-up-abm-accounts`:

### Goals
1. Identify upsell/expansion opportunities with existing customers
2. Reactivate stalled opportunities
3. Open conversations with new accounts
4. Identify engaged accounts for trigger-based outreach
5. Gain insights into specific markets/verticals
6. Co-create thought leadership content with buyers
7. Build credibility through industry influencers

### 6-Week Structure
**Week 1-2**: Strategy - Define angle, create participant list, write questions
**Week 3-4**: Outreach - Interview existing customers, reach out to prospects
**Week 5-6**: Activation - Compile report, host webinar, personalized follow-up

### Research Brief Components
- Research purpose and value to participant
- 100% confidential participation
- Topics to discuss
- Process (participation → interview → analysis → publication)
- Company credibility section with logos
- Host bio and headshot

### Interview Question Groups
- **Challenges**: Current pain points, priorities, impact
- **Current State**: How they solve today, tech stack, team access
- **Measurement**: Data accuracy scores, compliance importance
- **Prioritization**: Budget allocation, timeline pressures

### Activation Options
1. **Webinar**: Present insights, Q&A, content hub access
2. **Personalized Solutions**: Mini-audits for accounts showing need
3. **Direct Mail**: Premium printed reports with personalized cover
