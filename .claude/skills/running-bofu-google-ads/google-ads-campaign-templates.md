## Reporting KPIs

### Weekly Review

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| CTR | >3% (brand), >2% (non-brand) | Refresh ad copy |
| Conv Rate | >3% (brand), >1.5% (non-brand) | Check landing page |
| CPA | <₹X | Adjust bids, negative keywords |
| Impression Share | >80% (brand) | Increase budget |
| Quality Score | >7 | Improve relevance |
