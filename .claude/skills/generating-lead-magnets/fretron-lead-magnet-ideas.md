# Fretron Lead Magnet Ideas

Specific lead magnet concepts optimized for Fretron's ICP and buyer journey.

## Lead Magnet Tiers

### Tier 1: Awareness (Low Commitment)

**Goal**: Email capture from cold traffic
**Friction**: Minimal (email only)
**Trust Level**: 0 → 1

---

#### 1.1 Freight Cost Benchmark Report

**Title**: "2025 Freight Cost Benchmarks for Indian Manufacturing"

**Format**: PDF Report (8-12 pages)

**Content**:
- Average freight cost per MT-km by industry
- Detention/demurrage benchmarks
- OTIF benchmarks by sector
- Cost breakdown: direct vs. hidden costs
- Regional variations in freight rates

**Why It Works**:
- CFOs love benchmarks
- Self-assessment opportunity
- Positions Fretron as data-driven

**CTA After Download**: "See how your costs compare"

---

#### 1.2 Logistics Efficiency Checklist

**Title**: "25-Point Logistics Efficiency Checklist for Manufacturing Companies"

**Format**: PDF Checklist (4 pages)

**Content**:
- Visibility checks (5 items)
- Cost control checks (5 items)
- Process efficiency checks (5 items)
- Technology readiness checks (5 items)
- Customer service checks (5 items)

**Why It Works**:
- Quick to consume
- Self-assessment tool
- Creates awareness of gaps

**CTA After Download**: "Score under 15? Let's talk."

---

#### 1.3 Industry-Specific One-Pagers

**Titles**:
- "Logistics Challenges in Steel Manufacturing (And How to Solve Them)"
- "5 Ways Cement Companies Are Reducing Freight Costs"
- "FMCG Distribution: The Visibility Problem No One Talks About"

**Format**: PDF One-Pager (2 pages)

**Content**:
- Industry-specific pain points
- Common mistakes
- Quick wins
- Success metrics

**Why It Works**:
- Highly targeted
- Industry-specific SEO
- Shows expertise

### Tier 2: Interest (Medium Commitment)

**Goal**: Qualified email capture
**Friction**: Email + 1-2 questions
**Trust Level**: 1 → 2

---

#### 2.1 Freight Cost Calculator

**Title**: "Calculate Your Freight Cost Optimization Potential"

**Format**: Interactive Calculator

**Inputs**:
- Monthly freight spend
- Number of shipments
- Current OTIF %
- Hours spent on tracking/week
- Detention charges/month

**Outputs**:
- Estimated annual savings potential
- Breakdown by category
- Comparison to benchmark
- Personalized recommendations

**Why It Works**:
- Interactive = engaging
- Personalized output
- Captures qualification data

**Tech**: Can be built as web calculator or spreadsheet

---

#### 2.2 Logistics Maturity Assessment

**Title**: "Assess Your Logistics Maturity: Free Diagnostic"

**Format**: Online Quiz (15-20 questions)

**Sections**:
1. Visibility & Tracking (5 questions)
2. Cost Control (5 questions)
3. Process Efficiency (5 questions)
4. Technology Readiness (5 questions)

**Output**:
- Maturity score by dimension
- Radar chart visualization
- Personalized recommendations
- Industry comparison

**Why It Works**:
- Comprehensive self-assessment
- Creates "gap awareness"
- Rich qualification data

---

#### 2.3 TMS Buyer's Guide

**Title**: "The Complete Guide to Choosing a TMS for Indian Manufacturing"

**Format**: PDF Guide (15-20 pages)

**Content**:
- What is a TMS (and what it's not)
- Key capabilities to evaluate
- Questions to ask vendors
- Implementation considerations
- Pricing models explained
- Red flags to watch for
- Evaluation checklist

**Why It Works**:
- Helps buyer's journey
- Establishes authority
- Vendor-agnostic (builds trust)

---

### Tier 3: Consideration (Higher Commitment)

**Goal**: Sales-qualified leads
**Friction**: Email + company info + schedule call
**Trust Level**: 2 → 3

---

#### 3.1 Free Dispatch Value Audit

**Title**: "Get a Free Analysis of Your Logistics Operations"

**Format**: Service + Report

**Process**:
1. Fill form with basic info
2. Submit 1 month of data
3. Receive detailed analysis
4. Walkthrough call

**Deliverable**:
- Current state analysis
- Benchmark comparison
- Opportunity quantification
- Recommendations

**Why It Works**:
- High value = high qualification
- Creates compelling business case
- Natural transition to sales

---

#### 3.2 ROI Model Template

**Title**: "Build Your TMS Business Case: ROI Template"

**Format**: Excel Template + Guide

**Content**:
- ROI calculation framework
- Input worksheets
- Assumption documentation
- Presentation-ready outputs
- Industry benchmarks for assumptions

**Why It Works**:
- Helps champion build internal case
- Establishes Fretron's value framework
- Educational selling

---

#### 3.3 Implementation Playbook

**Title**: "TMS Implementation Playbook: What to Expect"

**Format**: PDF Playbook (12-15 pages)

**Content**:
- Typical implementation timeline
- Team requirements (your side)
- Common challenges and solutions
- Change management tips

**Why It Works**:
- Reduces anxiety (fear of change)
- Demonstrates expertise
- Builds confidence

## Lead Magnet Landing Page Framework

### Headline Formula

**Template**: "[Outcome] for [Audience]: [Specific Promise]"

**Examples**:
- "Freight Cost Benchmarks for Indian Manufacturing: See How You Compare"
- "Logistics Efficiency Checklist: 25 Ways to Reduce Waste"
- "Free Audit: Discover ₹X Lakhs in Hidden Freight Savings"

### Page Structure

```
HEADLINE
[Outcome-focused, specific]

SUBHEADLINE
[What they'll get and why it matters]

HERO IMAGE
[Visual of the lead magnet]

BULLET POINTS (3-5)
✓ What's inside point 1
✓ What's inside point 2
✓ What's inside point 3

FORM
[Email, Name, Company (optional)]

SOCIAL PROOF
[Customer logos, download count, testimonial]

PREVIEW
[Sample page or table of contents]
```

### Form Fields by Tier

**Tier 1 (Awareness)**:
- Email only
- Maybe first name

**Tier 2 (Interest)**:
- Email
- First name
- Company name
- 1 qualifying question (shipments/day, role)

**Tier 3 (Consideration)**:
- Email
- Full name
- Company
- Role
- Phone
- Qualifying questions (2-3)

## Lead Magnet Promotion Channels

### Organic

1. **Blog CTAs**: Inline and end-of-post offers
2. **Exit Intent**: Popup on high-traffic pages
3. **Resource Page**: Dedicated library
4. **LinkedIn Posts**: Share with direct link

### Paid

1. **LinkedIn Ads**: Sponsored content to ICP
2. **Google Ads**: Capture high-intent searches
3. **Retargeting**: Site visitors who didn't convert

### Outbound

1. **Cold Email**: "Thought this might be useful"
2. **ABM Warmup**: Value delivery touchpoint
3. **Event Follow-up**: "Here's what we discussed"
